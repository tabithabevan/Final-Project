WHAT'S THE GAMEPLAN?

Resources for the retiring collegiate athlete

What We Know:

  • 57.3% of former collegiate athletes attach negative sentiments to their transition out of sport

  • Premature athletic retirement, strong athletic identity, and weak support systems increase psychological challenges in retired collegiate athletes

  • Nearly 40% of retired collegiate athletes surveyed report that their university did not offer specific athletic retirement resources

  • Retired collegiate athletes are indifferent about the effectiveness of their university athletic retirement resources

What We Do:

  • Provide diverse athletic retiremenet resources to address unique and varied challenges of retired collegiate athletes

  • Partner with universities to help facilitate positive transitions out of collegiate athletics

  • Connect retired collegiate athletes with people and institutions who will help them acheive their goals outside of sport
